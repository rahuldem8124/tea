TEA TECH: INTEGRATED FACTORY INTELLIGENCE & COMMERCE SYSTEM
===========================================================

PROJECT OVERVIEW
----------------
This platform is a comprehensive, production-grade solution designed to modernize the end-to-end operations of a tea factory. It bridges the gap between industrial processing and digital commerce through a high-performance, real-time interface.

CORE WORKFLOW & CAPABILITIES
---------------------------

1. RAW LEAF INTAKE (THE SOURCE)
Detailed tracking of leaf collection from farmers. Each entry captures weight, moisture content, and quality grading (Fine Plucking %) to ensure the highest standards from the start.

2. PRECISION PROCESSING
A live production pipeline that monitors batches through every stage: Withering, Rolling, Fermentation, and Drying. 
* Integrated "Fermentation Monitor" provides real-time environmental data (Temperature/Humidity) with status alerts to maintain optimal conditions.

3. QUALITY GRADING & PACKAGING
Post-production analysis where tea is categorized into market grades (BOP, PEKOE, etc.). The system tracks "Outturn Percentage" and "Waste Monitoring" to optimize factory efficiency.

4. LOGISTICS & SUPPLY CHAIN
A GPS-independent dispatch board tracks truck movements from the factory to regional godowns. Real-time status updates (In-Transit, Delayed, Delivered) provide full visibility into the distribution network.

5. GODOWN & INVENTORY MANAGEMENT
Regional warehouse monitoring with automated "Low Stock" alerts. Includes a "Retail POS" interface for direct sales and inventory adjustments.

6. FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT
A centralized ledger handles dual-flow accounting:
* Farmer Payouts: Processing payments for raw leaf based on weight and quality.
* Buyer Invoices: Managing bulk sales and revenue tracking.
* Micro-loans: Issuing and tracking advance payments/advances to farmers.

7. THE CLIENT PORTAL (B2C COMMERCE)
A premium, customer-facing digital storefront.
* Product Catalog: Visual browsing of tea grades with detailed descriptions.
* Shopping Experience: Seamless cart management and secure checkout flow.
* Customer Dashboard: Order history and real-time shipment tracking for buyers.

TECHNICAL ARCHITECTURE
----------------------
* Frontend: Next.js 15 & React 19 (Server-side rendering for speed and SEO).
* Design: Tailwind CSS 4 with a "Glassmorphism" aesthetic for a premium, futuristic feel.
* Data: Recharts for real-time production trends and lucrative financial analytics.
* Performance: Optimized with Framer Motion for smooth, high-end micro-interactions.

SUMMARY
-------
This is not just a dashboard; it is an Operating System for the modern tea industry, designed to maximize yield, minimize waste, and provide a world-class buying experience for clients.
